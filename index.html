Chilli Panneer

Description

Spicy Indian cottage cheese cubes in sweet, tangy sauce - Indo-Chinese comfort food!

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Spicy Sauce
  • Vegetable | Aromatics

Preparation

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes.
  2. Prepare the spicy sauce by mixing soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar.
  3. Sauté vegetables and aromatics in a pan.
  4. Add the paneer cubes and sauce to the pan and cook until heated through.
